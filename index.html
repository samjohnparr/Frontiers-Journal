Aims & Scope

Frontiers in Multidisciplinary Research and Development (FMRD) is committed to rapid, rigorous peer review and open access dissemination of research that advances knowledge and practice across domains. We welcome submissions in (non-exhaustive): mathematics and data science, engineering and technology, health and life sciences, environmental and earth sciences, education, economics and social sciences, arts and humanities, and policy studies. Interdisciplinary, replication, and negative-result studies are encouraged.

  • Article types: Research Articles, Systematic Reviews, Short Communications, Registered Reports, Data Papers
  • Peer review: Double-blind; registered reports: stage 1 + stage 2
  • Open research: Data & code availability statements required; preprint-friendly
  • Ethics: COPE-aligned policies; ICMJE for medical research

Instructions for Authors

  1. Prepare your manuscript using the FMRD template (IMRaD, APA/Harvard/Vancouver—choose one consistently).
  2. Include an abstract (200–300 words), keywords (3–6), ethical approvals (if applicable), data/code availability statement, and funding disclosure.
  3. Deposit data/code in a trusted repository (e.g., OSF, Zenodo, GitHub) and include persistent links.
  4. Submit via OJS: Submit Manuscript.
