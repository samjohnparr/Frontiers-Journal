Submit a Manuscript

Choose one of the options below. We accept Original Research, Systematic Reviews, Short Communications, Registered Reports, and Data/Software Notes.

Download Template (DOCX) Instructions for Authors

Option A: Email Submission

Fill in the brief details below and click Compose Email. Your mail app will open with a pre-filled subject and body. Attach your manuscript (DOCX or PDF), figures, and any supplementary files.

Use your own subject/body

Send to: editor@fmrd-journal.org • Allowed files: .docx, .pdf, .zip (supplementary). If files are large, upload to OSF/Zenodo/Google Drive and include the link.

Option B: Google Form

If you prefer an upload form, use our Google Form. You can attach files directly (if enabled) or paste a link to a folder (Drive/OSF/Zenodo).

Open Google Form Download Template
  • Sign in with a Google account to upload files (if required by your form settings).
  • File naming: FMRD_[SurnameYYYY]_Manuscript.docx, FMRD_[SurnameYYYY]_Figures.zip.
  • Also include a plain-text cover letter in the form or as a separate file.

Submission Checklist

  • Manuscript follows the FMRD template and IMRaD structure.
  • Abstract (200–300 words) and 3–6 keywords included.
  • Ethics approval and consent statements (if applicable).
  • Funding and conflict-of-interest statements.
  • Data/Code availability statement with link (OSF/Zenodo/GitHub).
  • Figures: high-quality (vector preferred) with descriptive captions.
  • References consistently formatted (APA, Harvard, or Vancouver).
