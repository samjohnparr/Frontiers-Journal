Open Access & Licensing

Last updated: September 2, 2025

FMRD is a fully open access journal. All articles are free to read on publication and are published under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) license unless stated otherwise on the article. Authors retain copyright and grant FMRD a non-exclusive license to publish and distribute the Version of Record.

Copyright and Rights Retention

  • Authors keep copyright. The published article states © Authors and the CC BY 4.0 license.
  • Authors may reuse the article in any medium with proper citation of the Version of Record and the DOI.
  • Third-party content included in the article must have permissions that allow open distribution or must be clearly credited with any restrictions.

Self-Archiving and Sharing

FMRD supports preprints and repository deposits. The following versions may be shared without embargo.

  • Preprint (version submitted before peer review): may be posted anywhere at any time. Please cite the preprint in the submission and link from the article after publication.
  • Author Accepted Manuscript (post-review, author’s accepted version): may be posted in institutional or subject repositories and personal websites on acceptance. Include the DOI link to the Version of Record and a note that this is the accepted manuscript.
  • Version of Record (publisher’s PDF/HTML): may be shared and deposited under CC BY 4.0 with the full citation and DOI.

Text and Data Mining

Non-commercial and commercial text and data mining of FMRD content is permitted under CC BY 4.0. Please cite the source and the DOI for mined articles. If you require large-scale access, contact editor@fmrd-journal.org.

Funding and Mandate Compliance

  • CC BY 4.0 satisfies most funder and institutional open access mandates.
  • Articles include machine-readable license metadata on the landing page and in Crossref deposits to aid compliance checks.
  • Authors should list funders and grant numbers in the manuscript and provide ORCID iDs where available.

Article Processing Charges

Submission fees: none. Article processing charges (APCs): none at present. If FMRD introduces APCs in the future, changes will be announced on this page at least 90 days in advance and will not apply to manuscripts already under review or accepted.

Reuse and Attribution

  • Readers may share and adapt articles for any purpose under CC BY 4.0, with appropriate credit, a link to the license, and an indication of changes.
  • Cite the article using the recommended citation on the landing page and include the DOI.
  • For any material not covered by CC BY 4.0, follow the credit line and obtain permission when required.

Preservation and Persistent Identifiers

  • Articles are assigned Digital Object Identifiers (DOIs) and registered with Crossref for persistent linking and metadata.
  • The journal maintains off-site backups and is evaluating long-term preservation services. Preservation provider details will be posted here when finalized.

Data and Code

FMRD encourages the deposit of datasets, code, and materials in trusted repositories with open licenses compatible with reuse. Include a Data and Code Availability statement in the manuscript and cite repositories with persistent identifiers.

Questions

For license questions, permissions, or large-scale reuse, contact editor@fmrd-journal.org.
