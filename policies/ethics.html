Publication Ethics & Malpractice

FMRD adheres to COPE guidance. Misconduct (plagiarism, fabrication/falsification, duplicate submission) is investigated and may result in rejection, correction, or retraction. All parties must declare conflicts of interest.

Author Responsibilities

  • Originality, accurate reporting, and appropriate citation
  • Authorship criteria and acknowledgments
  • Ethics approvals (where applicable) and data availability

Editor & Reviewer Responsibilities

  • Confidentiality, impartiality, and conflict disclosures
  • Constructive, timely peer review
  • Handling concerns post-publication
