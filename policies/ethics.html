Publication Ethics & Malpractice

FMRD follows COPE guidance. Misconduct (plagiarism, data fabrication, duplicate submission) is investigated and may lead to rejection or retraction. Conflicts of interest must be disclosed.

Author Responsibilities

  • Originality and accurate reporting
  • Appropriate authorship and acknowledgments
  • Data availability and ethics approvals

Editor & Reviewer Responsibilities

  • Confidentiality and impartiality
  • Conflicts of interest disclosure
  • Timely, constructive reviews
