Peer Review Policy

FMRD operates double-blind peer review. Typically two expert reports are required. Registered Reports follow Stage 1 (protocol) and Stage 2 (final manuscript) review.

Timelines

  • Initial editorial screening: ~1 week
  • Peer review round: ~3–5 weeks
  • Decision outcomes: accept • minor • major • reject
