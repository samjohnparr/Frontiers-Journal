Aims & Scope

Frontiers in Multidisciplinary Research and Development (FMRD) advances rigorous, openly accessible scholarship across science, engineering, health, social sciences, education, and the humanities. We welcome original research, reviews, short communications, registered reports, and data papers.

Areas of Interest

  • Mathematics, Statistics, and Data Science
  • Engineering, Computing, and Technology
  • Health & Life Sciences; Public Health
  • Environmental & Earth Sciences
  • Education; Economics & Social Sciences
  • Arts, Humanities, and Policy Studies

Editorial Principles

  • Quality & Integrity: Double‑blind peer review; COPE‑aligned ethics.
  • Open Research: Data/code availability statements; preprint‑friendly.
  • Diversity of Results: Replications and negative results are welcome.
