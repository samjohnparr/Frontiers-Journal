Instructions for Authors

  1. Use the FMRD manuscript template (IMRaD). Choose one consistent reference style (APA, Harvard, or Vancouver).
  2. Include abstract (200–300 words), 3–6 keywords, ethical approvals (if applicable), and funding disclosure.
  3. Provide a data/code availability statement and deposit materials in OSF, Zenodo, or GitHub.
  4. Ensure figures/tables are high-quality (vector preferred) and have descriptive captions.
  5. Submit via OJS: Submit Manuscript.
